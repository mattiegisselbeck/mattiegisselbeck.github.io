About

I'm currently a graduate student within the MGIS Program at the University of Minnesota, and working as a graduate research assistant for forest resources at the University of Minnesota. Prior to the MGIS Program, I studided psychology with an emphasis on sustainability at the University of St. Thomas.

Within the MGIS Program, my studies are currently focused on web design, spatial data science, graphic design, and machine learning. My primary areas of interest involve utilizating spatial data science or machine learning methods to solve problems related to natural resource management, human moblity, and environmental justice.
